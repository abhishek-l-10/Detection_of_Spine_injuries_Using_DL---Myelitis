Spinal Cord

The spinal cord is essential for transmitting sensory information to the brain and motor signals to the body, coordinating reflex actions, regulating autonomic functions, and facilitating complex movements. It also provides structural support and protection within the vertebral column, crucial for overall body function and coordination.
